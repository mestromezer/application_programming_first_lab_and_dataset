Чистый лист. Природа человека. Кто и почему отказывается признавать ее сегодня



I believe the book is of very high quality and erudition. But the most relevant point is that Pinker tries to devastate stable stereotypes about human beings. These stereotypes have evolved in a humanistic context but their 'good intentions' contradict scientific knowledge. Thus, we should listen to science to overcome some deadlocks.
